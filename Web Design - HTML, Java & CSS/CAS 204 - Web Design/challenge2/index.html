Who Will Win?!

Given the popularity of both unicorns and robots, it should come as no surprise that these two factions would eventually do battle. Fighting for the hearts and minds of humanity, they must gather their wits and weaponry to discover which is the most powerful. Will the unicorn skewer his opponent with his magical horn? Perhaps the robot will utilize LAZOR BEEMS for a scorching attack!

Things to consider:

  • Robots are quite fast
  • Unicorns are suprisingly clever
  • Some robots have the ability to reconfigure their weaponry systems based on previous experiences (learning algorithms)
  • Unicorns can make flowers wherever they step. Flower Power!
