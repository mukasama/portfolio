    • History and Culture!

    • Uganda is located on the East Coast of Africa next to Kenya, Tanzania, South Sudan, Ethiopia, Rwanda and DRC. The largest kingdom in Uganda is Buganda from the Bantu population. During the 1800's Uganda was ruled by the British and became independent in October, 9 1962. The president of uganda is Yoweri Museveni.

    Geography, Food & Music!

    • The weather in Uganda is hot and humid with a chill breeze. The staple food of Uganda is Matooke which is similar to banana's. The main airport in uganda is Entebbe (EBB). Uganda uses the shilling as there curreny where 1 dollar is equal to 2,000 shilling. If you are going for travel, the best place to go to is Jinja, that has a rich history, different activites such as quad biking, nungee jumping and rafting.
