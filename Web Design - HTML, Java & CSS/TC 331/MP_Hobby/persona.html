About Me!

  • Mimi is a 23 year old lady from Milwaukee, Wisconsin. She has just graduated from University of Wisconsin with a Bachelor of Arts degree in International Relations. Mimi has always had a passion about traveling as she used to go on study abroad trips during her college years. However, now that she has graduated she has decided to take a year off and travel around the world. Her first place to visit is Uganda. Her very good friend is from there and recommended her to go visit. She can use this application to learn more about Uganda, as it links her to other sites where she can book her plane ticket, compare hotel and airline prices and also signup for tours dates and locations when she arrives in Uganda.
